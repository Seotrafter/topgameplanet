HOW TO PLAY

Touch the screen and move to avoid enemy attacks, shoot back and destroy them to save the galaxy. Simple gameplay suitable for all ages with increasingly challenging levels.

  • Touch & Move

    Navigate your ship across the screen to dodge incoming enemy fire

  • Auto-Fire

    Your ship automatically fires - focus on positioning and strategy

  • Special Attacks

    Tap to activate devastating special attacks when your power meter is full
